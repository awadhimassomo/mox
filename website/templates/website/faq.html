Frequently Asked Questions

Delivery Questions

What areas does Mo-Express deliver to?

Mo-Express currently delivers across major areas in Tanzania, including Dar es Salaam, Mwanza, Arusha, and other select urban areas. We're continuously expanding our service areas.

How long does delivery take?

For gas deliveries, we aim to deliver within 1-2 hours of order placement. Grocery and package deliveries typically take 2-3 hours depending on your location and current demand.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM every day of the week, including weekends and most public holidays.

What types of transport do you use for deliveries?

We use various transport modes based on delivery needs and location. This includes Boda (motorcycles), Guta, Kirikuu, and Kenta vehicles for different sized deliveries and areas.

Order Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our mobile app, website, or by calling our customer service. We recommend using the app for the fastest and most convenient experience.

Can I schedule a delivery for a later time?

Yes, you can schedule deliveries up to 7 days in advance through our app or website.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive real-time updates through the app, including rider assignment and live location tracking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money), credit/debit cards, and cash on delivery.

Business Partner Questions

How can my business partner with Mo-Express?

Register your business through our website or app. After registration, our team will review your application and contact you to complete the onboarding process.

What types of businesses can partner with Mo-Express?

We partner with gas suppliers, grocery stores, restaurants, retail shops, and any business that requires reliable delivery services in Tanzania.

What are the fees for business partners?

Our partnership fees vary based on your business type, volume, and specific requirements. Contact our business team for a customized quote.

Rider Questions

How can I become a Mo-Express rider?

To become a rider, you need to register through our rider registration page, have a valid driver's license, own or have access to a vehicle (Boda, Guta, Kirikuu, or Kenta), and pass our safety and background checks.

What are the requirements for different transport modes?

Each transport mode has specific requirements. Boda riders need a motorcycle and valid license. Guta, Kirikuu, and Kenta drivers need the appropriate vehicle classification and licenses for their vehicle type.

How do riders get paid?

Riders receive weekly payments through mobile money platforms. Earnings are based on deliveries completed, distance traveled, and applicable bonuses.
